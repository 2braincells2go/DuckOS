Thank you, everyone. And I mean it.

Skyhax holds a special place in my heart. The years spent crafting DuckOS, from v4 (I can't even remember why I started with 4) to v5 (a monumental achievement, all done without a template), and finally to v6, have been a journey I cherish. But, as much as I hate to admit it, I can't keep up with the work anymore. Next year, I'm stepping into high school, and I already have two high school courses in eighth grade. It's not something to brag about, as most kids are already handling three high school courses. However, my grades... they are not good at all. In the US, high school course grades impact your college application. Skyhax is devouring both my time and money that could've been invested in improving those grades, something my parents and I don't approve of.

I can't express how grateful I am for the journey Skyhax has taken me on. It all started in sixth grade when I was building Carrd sites and has led to me earning my Responsive Web Design certificate and fostering a fantastic community on Discord. It was an incredibly tough decision, but I've decided to put Skyhax development on hold for now. The site, servers, and proxies will still be up, and everything will remain free of charge. Work on Skyhax and DuckOS will resume after a substantial (and hopefully positive) bump in my next report card.

I understand this might not impact you as a user for now, but when I officially start high school, I'll likely have no time left for Skyhax. Extra classes imposed by my parents, peer pressure to socialize, and the growing pile of homework and assignments will consume my days. That's why this letter is here. After careful consideration, I've realized that Skyhax is on a downward spiral, and I've lost all motivation to continue. We've already weathered two major setbacks, and our community is rapidly falling apart. You can see how the deletion of a 400-member Discord server has affected our mood and traffic, leaving us with fewer active members.

After a great deal of thought, I've come to a decision: I will either sell Skyhax or entrust it to one of my close friends. The price is negotiable, with no fixed minimum or maximum. If you're interested, we can discuss it at admin@skyhax.lol.

I want to extend my heartfelt thanks to everyone who has shown appreciation and given DuckOS a try. In three years, we've grown to about 600 Discord members in total, including the two deleted servers. We've achieved 46.78k site visits and used 86.2GB of encrypted bandwidth, handling 118.14k site requests across all our domains (Kasm servers excluded).

I can't express how genuinely happy I am to have built this incredible community and shared DuckOS with so many. Thank you from the bottom of my heart for the support, encouragement, and camaraderie you've offered throughout this journey. It's been an amazing experience, and I'm incredibly grateful to each and every one of you.

I hope the new owner of Skyhax will take good care of everyone.



With sincere apologies and heartfelt appreciation,

Arthur Jiang
